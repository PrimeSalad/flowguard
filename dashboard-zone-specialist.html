Zone Investigation Guidelines

Standard Safety Protocol for Site Inspection

Always wear high-visibility vests and protective gear. Coordinate with the local barangay office before entering private properties or gated communities in Boac.

Reporting Illegal Connections

Document the connection with at least 3 photos from different angles. Do not confront the resident; file the report immediately through the "Illegal Usage" category in the Start Inspection tool.

Handling Major Main Pipe Bursts

Immediately request for valve isolation from the Technical Team. Mark the area with safety cones and update the status to "Critical" in the dashboard.
