Client submits inquiry/complaint
Inquiry/complaint is recorded in the Water Information System / Complaints file.
Zone Specialist is notified with complaint details.
General Manager reviews requirements and coordinates schedule, suppliers, contractors, in-house team, and inventory.
Purchase request is prepared when materials are needed.
Delivered items are checked and accepted.
Materials are issued to the relevant team/contractor.
Inventory is conducted and inventory reports / return slips are prepared.
Repair investigation is completed and details are sent to the relevant department.
Materials are estimated for the repair.
Customer is informed of repair details.
Job order is prepared and schedule is assigned to the in-house team.
Material availability is checked.
Material request list is updated.
MRF is approved and materials are issued; inventory is updated.
Issues/concerns are reviewed and, if needed, posted as service advisory/social media update.
