Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report damaged tools?

Log it under the "Material Requests" section and select "Tool Replacement". You must surrender the damaged tool to the Inventory Officer before receiving a new one.

What if a job takes longer than scheduled?

Use the "Update Progress" button on your job order and select "Delayed/Paused". Include a brief reason (e.g., hard ground, bad weather) so the dispatch team can adjust timelines.

Safety Protocol for Main Line Repairs

Ensure the area is cordoned off. Do not proceed with excavation until the zone specialist confirms the water flow has been successfully isolated at the nearest main valve.
