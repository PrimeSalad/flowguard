Standard Operating Procedures (SOP)

Receiving Materials

  1. Check delivery against Purchase Order (PO) reference.
  2. Inspect items for physical damage or manufacturing defects.
  3. Verify quantity received matches the delivery receipt.
  4. Accept shipment in the system to update digital inventory.
  5. Log any discrepancies immediately in the audit section.

Issuing Stock (MRF)

  1. Validate the MRF ID and requesting technician's identity.
  2. Ensure items requested are available and approved by GM.
  3. Pick items from warehouse and record serial numbers if applicable.
  4. Mark MRF as "Issued" in the dashboard.
  5. Hand over materials and ensure technician signs the physical log.

Low Stock Alerts

When an item hits the "Critical Low" threshold (e.g., < 10 units for Pipes), the system will flag it as Action Required.

Procedure: Notify the General Manager or initiate an Order Replenishment directly via the Materials list.
